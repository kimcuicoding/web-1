About me

I did a lot of things, maybe too many.

1/ Rebel

In the salad days of my youth, I fell head over wheels for skateboarding and turned into the unofficial paparazzo for my gang of merry misfits. Around the same tick of the clock, I got lured into the siren songs of gangster beats and the illicit charm of graffiti art. You could say I was less of a model citizen and more of a delightful scoundrel.

2/ Math wiz

As time marched on, I found myself wandering into the hallowed halls of university to wrestle with Mathematics, utterly bewitched by the sheer elegance of numbers, ratios, and patterns. It was there, amidst the arithmetic and algebra, that I was crowned with a master's degree in pure mathematics, as if by magic.

3/ Businessman

Then, my curiosity got hijacked by the world of machine learning, startups, and the thrilling rollercoaster of entrepreneurship. I threw myself into a few ventures, and even cobbled together a little business on the side. Along the way, I soaked up a veritable ocean of knowledge about technology and mastered the art of keeping an open mind.

4/ White collar

Now, I've landed in the big leagues, working at a colossal, globe-trotting professional services firm. Here, I'm the go-to guru for some of the most gargantuan private equity titans, guiding them through the labyrinth of tax due diligence and crafting cunning strategies for mergers and acquisitions.
